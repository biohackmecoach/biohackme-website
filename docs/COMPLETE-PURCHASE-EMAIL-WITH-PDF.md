## Troubleshooting

### Email Didn't Send After Purchase

**Check:**
1. Automation is **Active** (not paused) in Mailchimp
2. Trigger tag is exactly: `masterclass-customer` (check spelling/capitalization)
3. "Who can enter" has no filters blocking subscribers
4. Check automation activity log for errors

**Quick fix:**
- Manually add tag `masterclass-customer` to the user's email in Mailchimp
- They should receive the email within 2 minutes

### Customer Says They Didn't Get Email

**Steps:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Search for their email in Mailchimp
4. Check if they have tag `masterclass-customer`
5. Manually send them the links while investigating:

```
Hi [Name],

Here are your Biohacking Basics Masterclass resources:

Watch Masterclass: https://www.loom.com/share/61af56a7d8f445a9a47b22e5e6b3b8e4

Enjoy!
Camilla
```

### Loom Video Won't Play

- Loom videos work in all modern browsers
- Ask customer to try different browser
- Check if they have popup blocker enabled
- Video link is public - no login required

### PDF Won't Download

- PDF is hosted at: https://www.biohackme.com.au/downloads/Biohacking-Basics-Masterclass.pdf
