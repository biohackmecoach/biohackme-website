## How to Add the Button in Mailchimp

1. In the email editor, click where you want the button
2. Choose **Button** block from the content options
3. **Button settings:**
   - Button text: `Watch Masterclass Now` or `Access Masterclass Now`
   - Link: `https://www.loom.com/share/61af56a7d8f445a9a47b22e5e6b3b8e4`
   - Background color: `#0EA5E9` (sky blue) or `#1E6BB8` (ocean blue)
   - Text color: `#FFFFFF` (white)
   - Border radius: `8px` (rounded corners)
   - Size: Large
   - Alignment: Center
